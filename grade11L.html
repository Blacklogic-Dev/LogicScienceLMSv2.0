🧬 Genetics

Weight: High

Genetics is the study of heredity. It explains how traits are passed from parents to offspring through genes and DNA.

🧠 Key Concept:

Genotype: Genetic code (e.g., Bb)
Phenotype: Physical trait (e.g., brown eyes)

💡 Tip:

Use Punnett Squares to predict probability of inherited traits.

🌍 Evolution

Weight: Medium

Evolution is the process by which species change over time through natural selection and adaptation to their environments.

📘 Example:

Natural Selection:
- Variation exists
- Advantageous traits help survival
- Passed on to next generation

💡 Tip:

Charles Darwin proposed the theory of evolution by natural selection.

🫀 Human Anatomy

Weight: High

Human anatomy studies the structure and systems of the human body such as skeletal, muscular, digestive, and circulatory systems.

🩺 Example:

Major Systems:
- Circulatory: Heart, blood vessels
- Respiratory: Lungs, trachea
- Digestive: Stomach, intestines

💡 Tip:

Know the major organs in each system and their main functions.
