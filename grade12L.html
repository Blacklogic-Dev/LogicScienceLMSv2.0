🌿 Ecology

Weight: High

Ecology studies the relationships between organisms and their environments, including ecosystems, food chains, and biodiversity.

📊 Example:

Producer → Primary Consumer → Secondary Consumer

💡 Tip:

Producers make food (like plants); consumers eat other organisms.

🧬 Biotechnology

Weight: Medium

This topic covers genetic engineering, cloning, DNA technology, and how organisms are modified for benefits.

🧪 Key Concept:

DNA + Plasmid (vector) → Recombinant DNA
Inserted into bacteria → Cloning

💡 Tip:

Plasmids are small circular DNA found in bacteria, used to carry genes.

🫀 Human Physiology

Weight: High

This includes the respiratory, circulatory, excretory, and reproductive systems, explaining how each maintains body function.

🧠 Example:

Heart → Arteries → Capillaries → Veins → Heart

💡 Tip:

Arteries carry blood away from the heart; veins bring it back.
