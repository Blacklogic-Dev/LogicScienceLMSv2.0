🏃‍♂️ Kinematics

Weight: High

Kinematics is the study of motion without considering its causes. It deals with position, velocity, and acceleration of objects over time.

📐 Equation:

v = u + at
s = ut + ½at²
v² = u² + 2as

💡 Tip:

Use correct units: m/s for speed, m/s² for acceleration.

⚡ Work, Energy & Power

Weight: High

Work is done when a force causes displacement. Energy is the ability to do work. Power is the rate at which work is done or energy is transferred.

🔋 Equations:

W = F × d
E_k = ½mv²
P = W / t

💡 Tip:

Work is scalar – it has magnitude only, not direction.

🔌 Electricity

Weight: High

Electricity is the flow of electric charge. It includes concepts like voltage, current, and resistance in circuits.

💡 Ohm’s Law:

V = I × R
# V = voltage (volts)
# I = current (amperes)
# R = resistance (ohms)

💡 Tip:

Current always flows from positive to negative in a circuit diagram.
